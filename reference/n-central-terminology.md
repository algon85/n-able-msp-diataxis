## Purpose
Provide concise definitions for commonly used N-central terms.

## Terms

* **Agent**: A small software service installed on a device which monitors system health and executes tasks locally.
* **Customer**: A top-level organizational unit representing a client in N-central.
* **Device Class**: A category used to group similar hardware types, such as Workstations, Servers, or Laptops, for policy application.
* **NOC (Network Operations Center)**: The central dashboard used by professionals to monitor clients and respond to alerts.
* **Probe**: A specialized service installed on a single device within a network to perform discovery and monitoring of non-agent devices, such as printers or switches.
* **Service Organization (SO)**: The highest administrative level in the N-central hierarchy, typically representing the MSP itself.
* **Site**: A secondary organizational level under a Customer, used to represent different physical locations or sub-departments.
